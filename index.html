Booking

How booking works

Straightforward. No mystery fees buried in the footer (see FAQ for travel and stairs).

1

Tell us the when & where

Send your venue, guest count, and hours through the quote form or call. We confirm we’re free and quote the package that fits.

2

Deposit holds the day

$100 deposit secures the date; it comes off your balance (due by or on the event day). We line up template art if you need custom strips.

3

We run the booth

We arrive for setup, run unlimited sessions during your booked time, and pack down clean. Prints in hand in about ten seconds per round.
