Why hosts call us back

The practical stuff, spelled out

  • Prints you can hand out: dye-sub strips in roughly 10 seconds—two copies per round so someone can keep a strip for the guest book.
  • Digital in the same night: email or text to phones; full gallery follows within about 24–48 hours.
  • Room for the whole table: about a dozen guests in one frame on the open-air setup—bigger groups than a phone on a stick.
  • Someone on site: we don’t drop off a crate and disappear; the attendant runs flow and fixes hiccups.
  • Packages that scale: from two-hour essentials to four-hour deluxe—see pricing.
